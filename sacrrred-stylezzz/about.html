Our Story

From Faith to Fashion

Founded in 2020 by a group of Christian creatives in Nairobi, Sacrrred Stylezzz began as a small project to create faith-inspired apparel for our local church community. What started with just a few hand-printed t-shirts has grown into a full-fledged Christian clothing brand serving customers across Kenya and beyond.

The name "Sacrrred Stylezzz" reflects our core belief that fashion can be both sacred and stylish. The extra 'r's represent our commitment to righteousness and our radical approach to combining faith with contemporary design.

Every garment we create is designed to start conversations about faith, inspire believers, and share God's love through the universal language of fashion.

Our Core Values

Faith-First

We begin every design with prayer and seek to glorify God in all we do. Our products are more than clothing - they're ministry tools.

Quality Craftsmanship

We use premium materials and ethical manufacturing because we believe excellence honors God and serves our customers well.

Community Impact

10% of all profits support local ministries and youth programs. Your purchase helps spread the Gospel in practical ways.

Meet Our Founder

James Mwangi

"God gave me a vision to create a clothing line that would serve as walking testimonies. Each Sacrrred Stylezzz piece is designed to spark conversations about faith and remind the wearer of their identity in Christ."

With a background in both fashion design and ministry, James leads our team with a passion for merging creativity with discipleship. When he's not designing new collections, you'll find him mentoring young creatives at our Nairobi studio.
